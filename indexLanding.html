Our Story

A blend of secret ingredients, strict attention to freshness and quality have been the foundation of the tradition of good cooking you will find at Mi Ranchito. It was Papa, though, who had the idea to involve the whole family and create a truly special restaurant. The entire family pitched in and helped in tback in 1983. Manuel and Senorina Armenta eventually shared the responsibility of running Mi Ranchito with their sons and daughters. Today our resturant is still a family business;

family-owned, family-operated, and family loved.

And with legacy each one of the kids has their own story, They also worked hard for what they wanted, which is what the owner did. We make authentic food, and getting to meet different people every day.
